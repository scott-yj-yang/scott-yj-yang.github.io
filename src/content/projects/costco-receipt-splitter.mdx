## What it does

A practical tool for splitting Costco receipts among a group of people. Upload or input a receipt, assign items to people, and get an instant breakdown of who owes what — including tax proration.

## Key features

- Item-by-item assignment to people
- Automatic tax and tip proration
- Clean summary output for sharing
- Handles shared items split between multiple people
