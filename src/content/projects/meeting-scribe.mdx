## What it does

Meeting Scribe automatically transcribes and summarizes meetings, extracting key decisions, action items, and discussion points. Built as a CLI tool that processes audio recordings and generates structured summaries using LLMs.

## Key features

- Audio transcription with speaker diarization
- LLM-powered summarization with configurable detail levels
- Exports structured notes with action items and key decisions
- CLI-first workflow that integrates into existing toolchains
