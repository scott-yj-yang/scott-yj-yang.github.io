## What it does

LatePenalty automates the calculation and application of late submission penalties for course assignments. Born from years of TA experience at UC San Diego, it removes the tedious manual work of tracking deadlines and applying grading policies consistently.

## Key features

- Configurable late penalty policies (percentage per day, grace periods, hard cutoffs)
- Batch processing of student submissions
- Integration with course management platforms
- Audit log for transparency in grading decisions
